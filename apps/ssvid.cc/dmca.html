DMCA / Copyright Claims

Copyright protection and claim procedures

Copyright Respect

SSVID respects the intellectual property rights of content owners and complies with applicable copyright laws. We expect our users to do the same when using our services.


Role of SSVID

SSVID is a technical tool that processes user-submitted links. We do not host, upload, store, or distribute any video or audio content. All content accessed through SSVID is provided by third-party platforms and remains under their control.


Filing a DMCA Notice

If you are a copyright owner or an authorized representative and believe that your copyrighted work has been infringed through the use of SSVID, you may submit a copyright claim by providing the following information:


• Identification of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed.
• Identification of the material or link that you believe is infringing.
• Your full name and contact information.
• A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the disputed use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
• A statement that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.
• A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized representative.
