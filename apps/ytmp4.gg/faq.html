Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about using YTMP4

General Questions

What is YTMP4?

YTMP4 is a free online tool that allows users to download YouTube videos for offline use. It works directly in your browser and does not require registration or software installation.

Is YTMP4 free to use?

Yes. YTMP4 is completely free and does not require payment, subscriptions, or account creation.

Do I need to install any software or extension?

No. YTMP4 works entirely online. You only need a modern web browser to use the service.

Usage & Formats

What formats does YTMP4 support?

YTMP4 supports downloading YouTube content in popular formats such as MP4 for video and MP3 for audio, depending on the selected option.

Can I download long YouTube videos?

Yes. YTMP4 supports downloading long YouTube videos as long as the content is publicly accessible.

Does YTMP4 support HD or high-quality downloads?

When available from the source, YTMP4 allows users to download videos in higher quality formats such as HD.

Privacy & Safety

Does YTMP4 store downloaded videos or files?

No. YTMP4 does not store, archive, or retain any downloaded files. All processing is temporary and files are delivered directly to the user's device.

Is YTMP4 safe to use?

Yes. YTMP4 does not require personal information and does not store user data related to downloads. Users can use the service anonymously.

Does YTMP4 track my download history?

No. YTMP4 does not track or store download history.

Copyright & Legal

Is downloading YouTube videos legal?

Users are responsible for ensuring that their use of downloaded content complies with applicable copyright laws and the terms of the original platform.

Does YTMP4 host or upload videos?

No. YTMP4 does not host, upload, or distribute any video or audio content. It only processes user-submitted links.

How can copyright owners contact YTMP4?

Copyright owners or authorized representatives may contact YTMP4 via email at contact.ytmp4cc@gmail.com to submit a copyright or DMCA-related request.

Getting Started

To start using YTMP4, simply visit the YTMP4 homepage, paste a YouTube link, and choose your preferred download option.
