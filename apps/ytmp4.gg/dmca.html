Submitting a DMCA Notice

Copyright owners or their authorized agents may submit a DMCA notice to request review of specific material.

A valid notice should clearly identify the copyrighted work, the relevant URL or resource, and include a statement confirming the accuracy of the claim and authority of the requester.

Upon receipt of a properly formatted DMCA notice, YTMP4 will examine the information provided and determine appropriate next steps.
