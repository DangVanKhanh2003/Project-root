Copyright & DMCA

SSTUBE.Net is committed to respecting the rights of copyright holders and complying with applicable intellectual property laws.

This page outlines how copyright-related concerns and DMCA notices are handled in relation to the use of the SSTUBE.Net service.

Nature of the Service

SSTUBE.Net operates as a technical tool that processes user-submitted links to publicly accessible content.

The platform does not host, store, or distribute video or audio files.

All content remains on external platforms and is subject to their respective terms, policies, and legal frameworks.

User Responsibility

All actions performed through SSTUBE.Net are initiated by users.

Users are solely responsible for ensuring that their use of the service complies with applicable copyright laws and regulations.

SSTUBE.Net does not monitor, control, or verify the legality of user-submitted URLs.

Copyright Concerns

If you are a copyright owner or authorized agent and believe that the use of this website is associated with unauthorized access to protected material, you may submit a formal complaint.

SSTUBE.Net takes such concerns seriously and will review them in accordance with applicable legal requirements.

Submitting a DMCA Notice

To submit a DMCA notice, please provide clear and complete information including:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work being claimed
  • The specific URL or material in question
  • A statement confirming your good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement confirming that the information in your notice is accurate
  • Proof of authority to act on behalf of the copyright owner

Review Process

Once a valid DMCA notice is received, SSTUBE.Net will review the information and determine appropriate action.

This may include restricting or disabling certain processing capabilities related to the reported content, where required by law.

Each request is evaluated based on the completeness and validity of the submitted information.
