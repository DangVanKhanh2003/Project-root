What Is M4A Format?

M4A is an audio container format using AAC (Advanced Audio Coding) compression. Developed by Apple, M4A provides superior audio quality compared to MP3 at equivalent bitrates, and is the default format for iTunes and Apple Music.

Why Choose M4A?

  • Better Quality Than MP3: AAC compression delivers clearer audio at same file size.
  • Apple Optimized: Native support on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Music.
  • Smaller File Size: More efficient compression than MP3.
  • Wide Compatibility: Supported by most modern devices and players.
