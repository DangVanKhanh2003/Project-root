Privacy Policy

At Y2MatePro, protecting your privacy is a core priority. This policy explains what data we collect when you use our YouTube download service and how we handle it responsibly.

Last updated: January 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

Y2MatePro operates on a privacy-first principle. We do not require account registration, we do not store your downloaded files, and we collect only minimal technical data necessary to operate the service securely and efficiently.

What Information We Collect

Technical Data (Automatic)

When you use Y2MatePro, our servers automatically record standard web traffic data to maintain service quality and security:

  • IP addresses for rate limiting and abuse prevention
  • Browser type and device information for compatibility
  • Requested URLs (video links you submit) for processing only
  • Access timestamps for performance monitoring
  • Referral sources to understand traffic patterns

This technical data is retained temporarily for operational purposes and is not linked to any personal identity.

Contact Information (Voluntary)

If you reach out to us via email for support or feedback, we receive your email address and message content. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared or sold.

How We Use Your Information

The limited data we collect serves specific operational purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Process video conversion requests and deliver download links
  • Security & Stability: Detect and prevent abuse, spam, and malicious activity
  • Performance Optimization: Identify slow queries and improve server response times
  • Usage Analytics: Understand aggregate traffic patterns to scale infrastructure appropriately
  • Bug Resolution: Diagnose technical issues reported by users

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or sale to third parties.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

Y2MatePro uses minimal cookies to ensure basic site functionality. These include session cookies for form persistence and preference storage (like language selection). We also use Google Analytics to measure site traffic, which sets its own cookies governed by Google's privacy policy.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality. Learn more about cookie management in your browser's help documentation.

Third-Party Services

To operate Y2MatePro, we utilize the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic analysis (Privacy Policy)
  • Hosting Providers: Infrastructure services with standard server logs

Each third party operates under its own privacy policy. We select partners that maintain strong privacy standards, but we do not control their data practices.

Data Retention & Security

We retain server logs for a maximum of 30 days, after which they are automatically deleted. Your downloaded files are never stored on our servers-conversions are processed in real-time and delivered directly to your browser.

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), regular security audits, and restricted access to any stored data.

Your Privacy Rights

GDPR (European Users)

If you're in the EU, you have specific rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your data from our systems
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format

CCPA (California Users)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of any sale of personal data (note: we do not sell user data)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at y2matepro.com@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Y2MatePro does not knowingly collect information from individuals under 13 years old. Our service is designed for general audiences and does not target children. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal information should contact us at y2matepro.com@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated through a notice on our homepage. Your continued use of Y2MatePro after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
