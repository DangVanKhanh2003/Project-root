Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit on YouTube video length?

There is no fixed limit on video length. Long videos and livestreams can be processed as long as the original video is publicly accessible. Longer videos may require additional processing time.

Can all YouTube videos be downloaded in 4K?

No. Only videos that are originally available in 4K on YouTube can be downloaded in 4K quality. If a video does not provide a 4K stream, other available quality options will be shown.

How long does the download process take?

Most videos are processed within a few seconds. Very long videos or videos with higher resolutions may take slightly longer depending on system load and video availability.

Why is the download sometimes slow or not working?

This may occur during peak traffic periods, when processing very long videos, or due to temporary limitations or changes on YouTube.

Which formats and qualities are supported?

The tool provides video downloads in MP4 format based on the original YouTube video quality. Audio-only downloads in MP3 format are also available as an optional choice.

Are downloaded videos or audio files watermarked?

No. The website does not add watermarks, advertisements, or modify downloaded video or audio files.

Do I need to create an account or install software?

No. The service works directly in your browser and does not require registration or software installation.
