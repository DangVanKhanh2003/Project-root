Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is SSVID?

SSVID is a free online tool that allows users to download YouTube videos for offline use. It works directly in your browser and does not require registration or software installation.

Is SSVID free to use?

Yes. SSVID is completely free and does not require payment, subscriptions, or account creation.

Do I need to install any software or extension?

No. SSVID works entirely online. You only need a modern web browser to use the service.

Usage & Formats

What formats does SSVID support?

SSVID supports downloading YouTube content in popular formats such as MP4 for video and MP3 for audio, depending on the selected option.

Can I download long YouTube videos?

Yes. SSVID supports downloading long YouTube videos as long as the content is publicly accessible.

Does SSVID support HD or high-quality downloads?

When available from the source, SSVID allows users to download videos in higher quality formats such as HD.

Privacy & Safety

Does SSVID store downloaded videos or files?

No. SSVID does not store, archive, or retain any downloaded files. All processing is temporary and files are delivered directly to the user's device.

Is SSVID safe to use?

Yes. SSVID does not require personal information and does not store user data related to downloads. Users can use the service anonymously.

Does SSVID track my download history?

No. SSVID does not track or store download history.

Copyright & Legal

Is downloading YouTube videos legal?

Users are responsible for ensuring that their use of downloaded content complies with applicable copyright laws and the terms of the original platform.

Does SSVID host or upload videos?

No. SSVID does not host, upload, or distribute any video or audio content. It only processes user-submitted links.

How can copyright owners contact SSVID?

Copyright owners or authorized representatives may contact SSVID via email at contact.ssvidcc@gmail.com to submit a copyright or DMCA-related request.

Getting Started

To start using SSVID, simply visit the SSVID homepage, paste a YouTube link, and choose your preferred download option.
