Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit on YouTube video length?

There is no limit on video length. Very long videos can still be converted, but longer videos may require more processing time.

How long does the conversion take?

Short videos usually convert within 2–3 seconds. Longer videos, especially those over one hour, typically take around 10–15 seconds.

Why is the conversion sometimes slow or not working?

This usually happens during peak traffic periods or when converting very long videos. Temporary limitations from YouTube may also affect the process.

Are MP3 files tagged or watermarked?

No. Downloaded MP3 files do not contain watermarks, ads, or additional tags.

Do I need to create an account or install software?

No. The converter works directly in your browser and does not require registration or software installation.

Is there a daily conversion limit?

No. There is currently no limit on the number of conversions per user.
