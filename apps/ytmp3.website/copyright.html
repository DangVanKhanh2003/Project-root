Copyright & DMCA

YTMP3 respects the rights of copyright holders and is committed to complying with applicable intellectual property laws.

This page explains how copyright-related concerns and DMCA notices are handled in relation to the use of the YTMP3 service.

Nature of the Service

YTMP3 operates as a technical tool that processes user-submitted links to publicly accessible content.

The platform allows users to download audio from YouTube by extracting sound from videos without hosting or storing any media files.

All content remains on external platforms and is subject to their respective terms, policies, and legal frameworks.

User Responsibility

All actions performed through YTMP3 are initiated by users.

Users are solely responsible for ensuring that their use of the service complies with applicable copyright laws and regulations.

YTMP3 does not monitor, control, or verify the legality of user-submitted URLs.

Copyright Concerns

If you are a copyright owner or an authorized agent and believe that the use of this website is associated with unauthorized access to protected material, you may submit a formal complaint.

YTMP3 takes such concerns seriously and will review them in accordance with applicable legal requirements.

Submitting a DMCA Notice

To submit a DMCA notice, please provide clear and complete information including:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed
  • The specific URL or material in question
  • A statement confirming your good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner
  • A statement confirming that the information in your notice is accurate
  • Proof that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner

Review Process

Once a valid DMCA notice is received, YTMP3 will review the submitted information and determine appropriate action.

This may include restricting or disabling certain processing capabilities related to the reported content where required by applicable law.

Each request is evaluated based on its completeness, clarity, and legal validity.
